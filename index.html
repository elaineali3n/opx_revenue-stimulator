Model notes — Recognized MRR is accrual-based: monthly plans recognize full price; annual plans recognize the discounted price 1/12 per month. Cash is monthly fees plus full upfront payments from new and renewing annual subscribers. Deferred revenue is the upfront annual cash not yet recognized; it unwinds 1/12 monthly. The starting annual base is assumed uniformly aged (≈half its term remaining). Annual plans are committed for 12 months (no mid-term churn) and renew at the retention implied by monthly churn, (1−churn)¹². Net cash = cash collected − fixed costs − CAC spend (CAC = new paid users × cost per acquisition). LTV uses gross margin, so it reflects contribution, not revenue. Expansion/contraction MRR is not modelled (no plan upgrades). ★ marks operating break-even.
